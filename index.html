NEW ORDER

Image Source

Let me preface this by saying that I am not an 80s kid. I'm what 80s kids call millenials, but for some reason our generation is fixated on the past.For me, New Order's music gives me nostalgia for a time period I never lived in. Whenever I listen to one of their songs, I'm transported to cul-de-sac neighborhoods, overcast skies and riding on my bike around the neighborhood.

New Order falls under the alternative/punk genre, and characteristic to its time period, their albums are chock-full of thick synths. Much of their music is marked by a punchy bassline while the lead singer, Bernard Sumner's foggy vocals swims through the instrumentals. New Order is not new, but it's certainly timeless.
